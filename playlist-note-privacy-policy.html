Playlist Note iOS App
Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

When playing each song, the song name keyword will be sent to search API of the corresponding music service before playing, to find the matching one. Check privacy policy of the music service.

When switching music service for a song to play with, the song name keyword will be sent to search API of those music services to check availability. Check privacy policies of the music services.

  1. Apple Privacy Policy
  2. Google Privacy Policy (YouTube API Services)
  3. Spotify Privacy Policy
  4. KKBOX Privacy Policy

We don't collect any information in your playlist notes.

Terms of Use

By using this app, you are agreeing to be bound by the terms of service of the music services:

  2. YouTube Terms of Service (YouTube API Services)
  3. Spotify Terms and Conditions of Use
  4. KKBOX Terms of Use
